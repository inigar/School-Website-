About Our School


The Global School is a leading educational institution in the NCR, Delhi, was established to provide quality education through the latest in educational aids, equipment and amenities. THE Global School, an initiative of Global Educational Trust is built on a sound and common understanding of what a high-quality school of the future should be like. A child is inherently a learner with a natural inclination to explore, discover and share that new-found knowledge with family and associates. In today’s age, for a child to grow up into a responsible and responsive citizen, indeed a sensitive global citizen, a whole new mindset is vital in imparting learning in school which addresses a child’s multifarious developmental needs. We deliver quality education, resulting in nurturing students, both in terms of knowledge and character.
